🌷 About Our Project 🌷



Inspiration

As avid listeners of music, we wanted to create an interactive online community for music lovers to both discover and share music that emphasizes women’s stories, experiences, and perspectives. Throughout the past, women in the music industry have faced many instances of underrepresentation, from lack of credit in their songwriting to gender-based objectification or harassment. Our hope is that our platform celebrates the artistry of women as talented producers, songwriters, singers, and musicians.



Features

Our website highlights three categories that reflect women centric themes within music: top female artists, empowerment, and hidden gems. We want to emphasize the talent and success of the most popular female artists today and how they use their art and influence to positively impact other women. Our website also offers a playlist of song tracks that empower and support women through meaningful lyrics and messages. Hidden gems are hard to find, so our website is introducing users to new and rising female artists that are swiftly climbing the charts.
